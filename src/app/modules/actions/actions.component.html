Transform your Health:

Small Changes, Big Results

Now that you know your BioAge, what next?

We can improve our BioAge by taking small steps towards better health. Research tells us to focus on these three aspects of our health:

Our Blood Sugar

A 1% reduction in blood sugar in those with elevated levels is associated with a 17-21% reduction in the risk of cardiac events.

Our Weight

A 5kg weight loss in those with a high BMI can reduce diabetes risk by 58%.

Our Blood Pressure

A 10% reduction in systolic blood pressure in those with elevated levels is associated with a 25% - 35% reduction in the risk of cardiac events.
